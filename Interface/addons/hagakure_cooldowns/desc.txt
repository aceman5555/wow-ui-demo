Main idea of addon - reducing amount of raid leader work on tracking cooldowns. In this time I know only on addon for tracking CDs - [[http://www.curse.com/addons/wow/track-cooldowns|Track Cooldowns]]. But it shows only CDs. 

This addon's goal - show all **available** cooldowns of raid depending of player's spec. So if u have restor druid, u will see tranquility, that has 3 min cd.

Addon creates container for each group of spells (that u track, so u can choose if u wanna track or not current spell), that u can move, increase width of it, change direction of aligment spell's frames. Method of sorting spells:
# Casting in current time spell (example - Aura Mastery)
# Available excellent CD (example - restor druid tranquility).
# Available good CD (example - balance tranquility).
# Available bad CD (example - feral's tranquility).
# Spells on CD.
# Spells of dead people.
# Spells of offline people.
Tracking specified in configuration number of groups.

If you want to help with others localizations or have good idea, how increase quality of addon, you can always contact me through my [[kazmin.oleg@gmail.com|e-mail]]

PS. By deffinition CDs of blood DK and feral druid show's as raid CD type 1, so if your tanks don't have 4 pieces of t13, u can always turn tracking off. CD of prot pal barrier - 3 min, so if your prot pal has 4 pieces, you should remember to subtract 1 minute from CD.
